About the "Cultures" Exhibition

Welcome to "Cultures," a virtual journey exploring the rich tapestry of human life through 37 compelling photographs.

This exhibition aims to showcase diversity, connection, and the infinite stories captured within each frame. We hope you enjoy the experience.

Curated by PhotoSoc - University of Galway

Venue: Gallery, The Quad, University of Galway, Ireland
